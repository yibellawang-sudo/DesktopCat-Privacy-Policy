Privacy Policy for Desktop Cat

Effective Date: March 4, 2026

Desktop Cat respects your privacy. This extension does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information or user data.

Information Collection

Desktop Cat operates entirely locally within your browser. The extension:

  • Does not collect personally identifiable information
  • Does not collect browsing history
  • Does not track user activity
  • Does not collect location data
  • Does not transmit data to external servers

Local Storage

The extension uses Chrome’s storage API solely to store user preferences and reminder settings locally on your device.

  • Selected cat type and customization preferences
  • On-screen position settings
  • User-created reminder times or intervals

This data remains on your device and is never transmitted, shared, or sold.

Third-Party Services

Desktop Cat does not use analytics services, advertising networks, or third-party tracking tools.

Changes to This Policy

If the extension’s data practices change in the future, this privacy policy will be updated accordingly.
